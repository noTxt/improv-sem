The "rules" of improv

... really depends on who you ask ...

To Sell Is Human

  1. Hear offers
  2. Say "yes and"
  3. Make your partner look good

Improv Encyclopedia

  1. Don't Deny
  2. Don't ask open ended Questions
  3. You don't have to be funny (smart)
  4. You can look good if you make your partner look good
  5. Tell a story

Brainwaves

  1. Show up fit and well
  2. Accept offers
  3. Allow yourself to be changed

bottom line ... imho

  1. focus on listening
  2. learn to detach from your ideas
  3. be aware of the group
  4. play!
